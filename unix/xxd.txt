xxd is sorta like hexdump

> echo "abcde" > x.txt
> hexdump -C x.txt
00000000  61 62 63 64 65 0a                                 |abcde.|
00000006
> xxd x.txt
0000000: 6162 6364 650a                           abcde.
>
-----------------------------------------------
Use of the -p flag gives just the hex:

> xxd -p x.txt
61626364650a
>

> xxd -p x.txt > x.hex
> cat x.hex
61626364650a
>
-----------------------------------------------
The -r flag isn't much good by itself (at least so far)
but with -p we get:

> xxd -r x.hex
> xxd -r -p x.hex
abcde
> echo "abcde" | xxd -p | xxd -r -p
abcde
>
-----------------------------------------------
So using -r we can go from hex to binary
and solve the problem I had with crypto prob #1
